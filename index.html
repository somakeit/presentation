Southampton Makerspace

A not-for-profit community meeting space and workshop

Presentation by @Benjie

What is a Makerspace?

hackerspace noun. 1. A community-operated workspace (also referred to as a hacklab, makerspace, or hackspace) where people with common interests, often in computers, technology, science, digital art or electronic art, can meet, socialise and/or collaborate.

What is a Makerspace?

  • Community
  • Collaboration
  • Inspiration
  • Learning
  • Teaching
  • Sharing
  • Hanging out
  • Eating
  • World-wide movement.

Why set up a Makerspace?

  • Geeks thrive in a community
  • Share big, expensive tools
  • And small tools too
  • Share knowledge and projects
  • Learn together
  • Gain inspiration
  • Try new things
  • Bounce ideas around
  • Storage.

Progress?
